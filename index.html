Part 2

Blockchain - the next revolution?

What is a blockchain?

  • decentralized ledger
  • secured and transparent
  • anonymous

How does it work?

  • Transactions are saved in blocks.
  • Blocks are verified and validated by the network.
  • Each block contains the hash of the previous block.

Is it secured?

  • Distributed on all nodes of the network!
  • Hash calculation is very difficult (hashcash proof of work)
  • Prevents double spending
  • Attack of the 51% (the longest chain is the right one)

Is it a revolution?

  • Decentralized means no third party. (banks? notaries? Uber? Airbnb?)
  • Transparency means trust.
  • Fraud is much harder.
  • Could save a lot of money!

Part 4

Ethereum - a different approach

What is Ethereum?

  • Born in 2013
  • Build a web without any third parties between customers and services
  • Uses blockchain and secured smart contracts
  • Uses Ether currency to pay the execution of these smart contracts

Hail to Smart contracts

  • Uses the Solidity language
  • Terms of contract can't be modified!
  • Executed automatically on a decentralized network
  • Transparent : everyone can verify their execution
